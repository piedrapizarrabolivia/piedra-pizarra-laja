Delivery Free delivery available to most mainland UK. Delivery fee will be charged outside free delivery areas. See our delivery section for more information.
Texture Riven with sawn edges
Thickness 22 mm nominal ( +/- 2-3mm calibration tolerance)
Handcut Sizes 1200x600 mm
Pack Size One full pack of 15.12 m2 contains 21 slabs; 10.8m2 pack contains 15 slabs; 7.2m2 pack contains 10 slabs; 5.04m2 pack contains 7 slabs. Quantity will vary depending on pack size.
Weight (kg) The full pallet is 1000kg
Coverage The full pallet will cover 15.12m2 (gaps are not included)
Applications We recommend that slabs are primed individually before laying. Please speak to your landscaper for more information.
Material CE Certified Indian Limestone
Primer Application Industry Standard primer must be applied to the back of the slab. 1 full tub will provide 8m2 coverage.
Joint-it Application Industry standard jointing compound to be applied with a 7 to 10mm gap. Estimated coverage of 15m2 to 18m2.

Black Limestone 1200x600 has smooth machine cut edges. - Most popular in the limestone range for a luxurious and modern feeling. It originates from a place called Kadapa in India hence also called as Kadapa Black.

The product weathers naturally to beautiful greys over time with weather, if you would like to preserve the colour of the stone, we recommend sealing it with any commercial sealant after 10-12 weeks of laying. Our Natural Stone is high quality for your patios, garden paving and landscaping projects from Direct Importers. All our products are suitable for British weather conditions and they are frost resistant.
